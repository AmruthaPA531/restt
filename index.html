About Us

Green Paradise is the oldest restaurant in Kerala, offering a variety of delicious and soul filling Indian cuisines to our customers. Established in the year 1980, we have been offering our services in Kochi. Our menu includes a wide assortment of Indian and International foods.

At Kochi, we have a well furnished restaurant with a capacity of 210 people. This restaurant is fully furnished with beautiful eye pleasing d√©cor and has air conditioned cabins as well as non air conditioned cabins for our customers to enjoy our food.

Our chefs are versatile and well qualified in offering the best food which has been tailor made to suit our Indian taste buds. We never compromise on quality and offer the best grade food which matches all the parameters set by food experts. We use only the best quality raw materials, which are fresh and healthy. Our professionals are prompt in their services.

We Green Paradise are one of the oldest restaurants offering delicious, tasty and saucy type food to our customers.
